How to Play

Game Objective

Race through traffic, avoid collisions, and survive as long as possible while collecting points. The longer you survive and the more cars you pass, the higher your score will be!

Controls

Movement

  • Left Arrow: Move Left
  • Right Arrow: Move Right
  • Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow: Brake

Game Controls

  • Space Bar: Start/Pause Game
  • R Key: Restart Game

Scoring System

  • Pass a car: +100 points
  • Speed bonus: Up to 2x multiplier
  • Time bonus: +10 points per second

Tips & Tricks

Beginner Tips

  • Start slow and focus on avoiding collisions
  • Stay in the middle lanes when possible
  • Watch for patterns in traffic movement

Advanced Strategies

  • Use brake to create gaps in traffic
  • Maintain high speed for score multipliers
  • Plan your moves several cars ahead
